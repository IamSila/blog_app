This write up is about converting a hex(base 16) to decimal(base10). The hex values will be so frequent in binary exploitation and reverse engineering challenges and is thus important to understand a thing of two about them.

The Challenge

    What is 0x3D (base 16) in decimal (base 10)?

The Solution

In my previous writeups, I talked about the importance of online tools and the impact they play in technology, especially with automation thus saving us so much time.

So, in that case we shall use Rapidtables

Choose from Hex and to Decimal and paste your hex value. Use your output to generate your flag

The flag

picoCTF{61}
